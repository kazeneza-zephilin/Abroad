# Real Numbers & Statistics

## Industry Scale
- **2 million+** people have participated in AIFS programs since 1964
- This demonstrates study abroad as mainstream, not niche

## Career Impact (from student experience)
- Cross-cultural communication is a must-have skill "across nearly every industry"
- Study abroad participants report being "stand-out candidates" on job applications
- International experience builds a "global network that opens doors you never expected"

_Note: While specific placement rates aren't provided by AIFS in this source, the consistent mention of career advantages across multiple student testimonies is worth noting._

## Timeline for Language Learning
- **1 semester** (Ketina's experience in Salamanca): conversational fluency with immersion + coursework
- Native speakers recognized her Spanish as fluent after spring semester abroad
- This is significantly faster than traditional classroom learning (typically 2-3 years for conversational fluency)

## Friendship Durability
- Study abroad alumni report close friendships "**even years after they've returned home**"
- Shared experience of navigating new environment fast-tracks connection formation
- Works for introverts and extroverts alike (Derek's story)

## Program Options
- **Fall & spring semesters** for degree credit
- **Winter & summer programs** to take additional credits (get ahead on graduation)
- Weekend travel opportunities for exploration

## Student Demographics
- Participants range from underclassmen to upperclassmen to recent graduates
- AIFS serves diverse student types (academics, internships, travel-focused)
- Programs available globally across 30+ countries

## Memorable Experience Rating
- Students consistently cite study abroad as "one of the most memorable experiences of their life"
- Alumni bring it up unprompted and enthusiastically when discussing college

_Note: These aren't formal statistics but represent themes across thousands of participant experiences documented by AIFS._
